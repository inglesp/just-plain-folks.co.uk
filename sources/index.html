Archives

  • The National Archive, Kew, contains information about James Williams and John Robinson on microfilm ADM 175/7(Part 2) to 175/9
  • Diary of Joseph Humphrey, blacksmith of Caister is held at the Norfolk Record Office, catalogue reference MC 1193/1, 807XB
  • Records of the Winterton Provident Friendly Society are held at the Norfolk Record Office, catalogue reference SO 63
  • The Scott Polar Research Institute Cambridge holds the unpublished diaries of James Nelson, an able seaman on the Investigator, and include his poem about James Williams and his ‘lanthorn’.
  • The Charles Parker archive, with recordings of the interviews that he made for the Radio Ballads, is in the Birmingham City Archives, Central Library, Birmingham.
  • Copies of the Standard newspaper are held in the British Library Newspapers, Colindale.
